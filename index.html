Seedling Games makes adventures and other content for D&D, 13th age, as well as standalone indie games and games for indie systems like BOLT. In addition, Seedling Games makes maps and other resources for GMs and for TTRPG writers producing commercial content. Seedling Games also started out as a personal blog about worldbuilding, which still continues both in blog posts and in her approach to adventure writing.

As well, she practices several pre-modern crafts including spinning, dyeing textiles, and blacksmithing. While she uses her interests to bring touches of authenticity to pre-modern fantasy worlds, she rejects ahistorical claims that historical fantasy is at odds with diversity.

Published Content - Maps

All maps allow for limited commercial reuse - you may use them in an adventure or similar product, but may not simply repackage and resell them unchanged.

This pack of watery outdoor maps includes a woodland scene that can become almost completely flooded, a ruin being flooded, two river scenes, one lake, and a cliffside path partially washed out by rain.
This pack of rural maps contains a mill by the water, a temple in the woods rebuilt over time, a two-story fort turned tavern, a smithy and butcher's by the river, a well near a mysterious circle in the woods, an overland map and some plot hooks associated with the map.
This pack of city maps contains a several story defensive tower with a small jail in the bottom; an enormous market with each section also as a standalone variant; a large manor.
