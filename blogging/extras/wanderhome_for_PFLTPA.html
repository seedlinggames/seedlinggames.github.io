Using PFLTPA with Wanderhome

If you want to use tokens instead of rolling to travel, then:

Get a token when you enter farmland or gentle wilds.

Spend a token to reach rough wilds, a barrier, or an unfriendly area. Put spent tokens in a pile.

Once your pile of spent tokens has at least 3, you can get a token and enter a town. Once it has 6, get a token and enter a city, or spend a token to enter the deep wilds.

Whenever you spend your last token, go to a waystation.

Demons in Wanderhome

A demon is a spirit that causes problems for people. They are often the manifestation of old conflicts between people, or with nature, or of other wrongs done. If you are playing without traumatized traits, you may want to consider not including demons.

In Wanderhome, the problem a demon causes is probably more subtle than mere violence, although violence could be part of the demon‚Äôs story: for instance, a ghost from the war that still tries to protect the old farmhouse (dead, feral).

Pick 1-2 of the following: furious, grieving, hurt, starving, dead, royal, miraculous, oracular, witchy.

In addition, you might want to add one of these traits: crafty, ambitious, cunning, venerable, resolute, raucous

Aesthetic elements:

  • An invisible presence
  • Regal and eerily glamorous
  • Fanged, clawed and feral
  • Many-faced, taking the form of an old friend or enemy
  • Intertwined with nature: storms, wilderness, fire, etc

Some things a demon can do:

  • Be unnerving or unsettling.
  • Reveal old wrongs.
  • Encourage someone to do something they‚Äôll regret. If they agree, give them a token.
